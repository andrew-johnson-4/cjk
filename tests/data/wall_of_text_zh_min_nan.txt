Tâi-pak-chhī Chèng-hú sī tī Tâi-oân ê Tiong-hôa Bîn-kok ti̍t-ha̍t-chhī Tâi-pak-chhī siōng-koân chân-kip ê hêng-chèng ki-koan; 
tī Tiong-hôa Bîn-kok Chèng-hú ê kè-kò͘ tiong, sī ti̍t-hat-chhī ê chū-tī hêng-chèng ki-koan. 
Tâi-pak-chhī Chèng-hú tông-sî mā hū-chek chip-hêng tiong-iong ki-koan úi-pān ê sū-hāng, 
i-ê chū-tī kàm-tok ki-koan sī Hêng-chèng-īⁿ. 
Tâi-pak-chhī Chèng-hú lāi-pō͘ siat ū 32-ê it-kip ki-koan, 112-ê jī-kip ki-koan, 12-ê khu-kong-só͘, 236-keng kok-kip ha̍k-hāu, 14-keng iù-tī-hn̂g, 
lēng-gōa iáu-ū keng-êng 3-keng chhī-êng sū-gia̍p ki-kò͘; 
pian-chè lāi ê oân-kang lâng-gia̍h lóng-chóng ū 7-bān gōa lâng.
